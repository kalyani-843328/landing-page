About the Architect

Brinda Somaya (born June 28, 1949) is a preeminent Indian architect, urban conservationist, and author known for bridging contemporary design with historical heritage. She is the founder and principal architect of Somaya and Kalappa Consultants (SNK), established in Mumbai in 1978.

Selected Projects

Historical Restoration & Conservation

Somaya is a leading figure in Indian urban conservation, specializing in preserving 19th-century colonial and 20th-century modern heritage.

Institutional & Educational Campuses

Her institutional designs often feature "courtyard" planning and sustainable material choices like local brick.

Social & Community Design

These projects reflect Somaya’s belief in the architect’s role as a "guardian" of the community. .
